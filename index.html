Project 05 — Medical AI & Imaging
Deep LearningAzure MLGrad-CAMClinical AI

Chest X-ray AI analysis pipeline

Designed and implemented an end-to-end AI pipeline for automated abnormality detection in chest radiographs — addressing a real radiology bottleneck of 800+ daily X-rays across three hospital sites with 12–24 hour reporting delays. The system preprocesses DICOM images, runs a fine-tuned ResNet-50 model, generates Grad-CAM explainability heat maps, and routes critical findings to on-call radiologists in under 2 minutes.

  • Built a DICOM preprocessing pipeline with CLAHE contrast enhancement, 512×512 standardisation, and automated quality filtering (~2% exclusion rate)
  • Fine-tuned ResNet-50 (ImageNet pre-trained) with class-weighted binary cross-entropy loss — achieving AUC 0.94, Sensitivity 91%, Specificity 86%
  • Implemented Grad-CAM heat map generation aligned with radiologist annotations in 88% of cases; false localisations traced to motion artifact and underexposure
  • Designed a 0–10 composite priority scoring system with clinical modifiers (ED +2, "stat" flag = max) and alert fatigue prevention mechanisms
  • Integrated with Azure Logic Apps for automated Teams notifications on high-confidence critical findings — end-to-end latency under 2 minutes
  • Documented pipeline for FDA 510(k) pathway including intended use statement, failure mode analysis, and phased 4-month clinical rollout plan
